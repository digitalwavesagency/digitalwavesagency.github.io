Creative Director

Julia Conoscenti

Julia Conoscenti is a hardworking, highly creative individual who received her Masters in PR & Advertising from the University of Southern California. As Creative Director, Julia oversees all creative strategies, as well as create, plan and execute and creative strategy for all clients. Julia plans out advertisements and uses DWA assets for promotional purposes and to aid in shaping brand standards. At the age of nine, Julia knew she was destined to be on the big screen after fully directing, producing and writing a screenplay that her brother and neighbors starred in. Since then, she’s guest starred in multiple shows on networks including Nickelodeon and CBS. Julia loves being able to embody human emotions and move people through media. Julia is passionate about the fact that Marketing and Advertising allows individuals to flex their creative muscles in a collaborative environment and not have to color inside the lines.
